our story so far

Where it began
The first day I noticed you

The first day, maja piluu — I still remember that black punjabi dress you wore the day I saw you first, and I couldn't take my eyes off you. At that moment I thought, "How lucky a man would be who gets a girl like her." Now I'm the one who's the luckiest in the world. I LOVE YOU BAE.

A test of trust
The day you stood up for me

When your mother's trust in you wavered over something that had nothing to do with us, you didn't panic — you stayed calm and told her I was different. You even remembered the small things I'd quietly done for you, like helping with your 12th grade fees. That day I understood how deeply you believed in "us." I LOVE YOU BAE.

April, imperfect and real
Even our fights taught me something

You've always cared more about my birthday than your own — that's just who you are. That April wasn't easy; your MBA admission stress boiled over into our first real "cold war," and we slept angry that night. But even that taught me something: we could disagree and still choose each other the next morning. I LOVE YOU BAE.

Through the distance
Still choosing you, every day

The distance never really touched us — we can't go a single day without talking or thinking of each other. Yes, we fight, a lot, but that's just part of the journey: the more you love, the more you fight. Once again — I LOVE YOU MAJA BABUUUUU.

Today
5+ years, and still my favourite person

Back when we first started talking on Snapchat, our very first conversation was about "snapstreaks." You asked how many days I wanted our streak to reach — I said "more than 500." We said okay, done — but thanks to our fighting skills, we never actually made it to 500 on Snapchat. Instead, we made it to 5+ years of streak in real life, just because of you. Just because of you and your unconditional love. I LOVE YOU.
